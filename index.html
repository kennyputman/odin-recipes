Homemade Salty Caramel Ice Cream

This luxurious ice cream is the ideal balance of sweet yet salty and rich yet light. It's custard-based, which means you have to separate a lot of eggs (six), but the thick and silky results are worth a little bit of fuss. Despite the rumors, making caramel is not hard, but it can be a little tricky.
